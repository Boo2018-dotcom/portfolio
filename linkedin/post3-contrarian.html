Hot Take
A watch isn't a
small phone.
I designed UPI payments for Wear OS.
My first mistake was treating it like one.
📱
Phone thinking
✕"Simplify" the phone flow
✕Show all options upfront
✕Type to input values
✕Designed for focused sessions
✕Confirm → Cancel → Done flow
⌚
Watch thinking
✓Design for physical triggers
✓Prioritise for the common case
✓Tap to confirm, not type
✓Glanceable, interruptible
✓One decisive tap = done
Every design decision shifted from "options" to "confidence."
Bhuvaneswaran B UPI on Wear OS · Product Designer
