Design Decision

Not one solution.
Two paths.
Zero keyboards.

Text keyboards and QR scanning were out. What remained: two complementary paths depending on who you're paying.

✕ Text keyboard Too small, error-prone on round display
✕ Voice input Awkward in public
✕ QR scan No camera on watch
✓ Numpad → mobile number New recipient, large-tap keys
✓ Saved contacts Repeat payment, one tap
The Insight
Mobile number = UPI ID.
Numpad for new. Saved contacts for repeat.
What UPI already supports
UPI ID ravi.k@okicici Long
Mobile no. 98XXXXXXXX 10 digits
What was ruled out

Text keyboards, voice input, QR scanning — none work on a circular watch display. The numpad is the right input surface for digits.

The solution
Saved contacts
Synced from phone app once. On the watch, every payment is a tap — not a keystroke.
