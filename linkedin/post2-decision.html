Design Decision

I deleted
the UPI ID
input field.

UPI IDs look like this:
firstname.lastname@bankname

Typing that on a 390px circular screen is nearly impossible. And after talking to users, the insight was obvious in hindsight.

Removed
ravi.kumar@okicici
Replaced with
RRavi PPriya AAmma
The Lesson
"Remove it" is almost always the right design decision.
